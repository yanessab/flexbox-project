Hi. I'm Yanessa.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who loves visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects, I can be found curled up with a mystery novel, exploring my city, searching for good eats and when possible, traveling.

I was born in Northern California, but raised in Mexico. First in Guadalajara and then at 5 years old we moved to Tijuana where my brother was born. My love for travel started when I was 18 and went to Paris for a summer French class. It was all downhill from there and I've had a constant itch for travel ever since.

For fun I love create things. I love making jewelry, weaving, practicing my calligraphy, coloring and anything else with a crafty twist. Sometimes I even love baking.

My latest obsession is making my own skincare products. I just love knowing what products are used and how they work. Also I get a sense of satisfacion being able to mix the concoctions together. It's like I'm a chemist for a minute.
